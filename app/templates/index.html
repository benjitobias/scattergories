Rules

  1. The list is used to remember your answers, spelling mistakes are acceptable IF the intended word is remembered correctly.
  2. When the letter isn't "T", an answer beginning with "The" doesn't count.
  3. Adjectives aren't allowed unless it has a significant change. For example, Panda and Red Panda are accepted. Car and Red Car are not. Neither are adverbs.
  4. The same word is not to be used twice on the list even if it has a different meaning. Drake the bird and Drake the musician is classed as the same.
  5. An answer is accepted unless challenged by another player. If challenged, the answer must be proven.
  6. Guessing is allowed but an example must be given. Billie is a name in a song, but which song? Other players are allowed to help.
  7. Disagreements go to a vote. If the vote is even, the point is given as people have voted in favour of an opponent.
  8. Benji is the boss. What Benji says, goes. Also, Abby-Jo has to send photos of Bump.
